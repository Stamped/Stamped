The Guide - what you want, when you want it.

Want to try something new? Look no further than The Guide, the visual and personalized way to discover the best new things, curated completely by people you trust.

The Guide integrates with a bunch of popular third-parties such as iTunes, Spotfiy, Rdio, Fandango, Netflix, OpenTable and Amazon, so it’s easy to listen to and download music, buy movie tickets, add movies to your queue, buy books, and even view restaurant menus, all without ever leaving Stamped.
